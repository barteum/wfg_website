About Walk For God

The Great Commission, One Step at a Time,
One Home at a Time, One Local Area at a Time.

Walk For God (WFG) is a Special Purpose Non-Profit Organization established with the singular mission of delivering the core message of Jesus Christ directly to the letterbox at no cost to Christian individuals or churches.

The structural backbone of WFG is the advanced Licensed Delivery and Tracking System developed by MailPost (mailpost.com.au). MailPost is recognized as a leader in Australian letterbox distribution, upholding the highest Star Google rating for a logistics business in Australia and offering a 99% distribution guarantee with full proprietary AI-Driven GPS Auditing for all commercial clients.

The founders of MailPost are dedicated Christians who have experienced the undeniable power of the letterbox medium. They established WFG to provide a platform where followers of Jesus could utilise this highly effective medium on a consistent basis — subsidising the administration and print production to ensure the proclamation of God's Word reaches every home in their local communities.
