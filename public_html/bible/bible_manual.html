2. Getting Started

By default, the app runs in Online Mode (Read-Only). To unlock the full potential, you must switch to Personal Mode.

Active Personal Mode

  1. Click the Settings Icon ‚öôÔ∏è at the top right.
  2. Under "General Settings", look for the "My Local Bible" section.
  3. Click Connect Local Folder.
  4. Select a folder on your computer where you want to store your Bible data.
  5. The app will initialize your personal database.
Why a Local Folder? This gives you physical ownership of your data (`bible_data.json` or `.csv`). You can back it up, move it, or open it in Excel!

3. Navigation & Reading

The interface is designed for focus and clarity.

Selecting Scripture

Use the three dropdown menus at the top:

  • Book: Select the book (e.g., Genesis).
  • Chapter: Defaults to Chapter 1 automatically for quick reading.
  • Verse: Optional. Scroll directly to a specific verse.

Reading Modes

Once a book is selected, you can scroll through the chapter. At the bottom of each chapter, use the "Next Chapter" button to continue reading seamlessly.

4. Search & Replace

The search bar is your command center for finding scripture.

Standard Search

Type any word or phrase into the search box and press Enter. Results will appear below.

  • Exact Match: Check this box to find exact phrases only.
Smart Search Scope:
  • If you select a Book (e.g., Genesis) and type text, it searches the Whole Book.
  • If you manually select a specific Chapter (e.g., Genesis 5), it searches Only that Chapter.

Global Replace

Want to change a word everywhere? (e.g., "servant" to "bondservant")

  1. Perform a search for the term you want to change.
  2. In the results header, click Replace All.
  3. A popup will appear. Enter the new term.
  4. Confirm the replacement. It updates all occurrences instantly.

5. Editing & Personalization

This is the core feature of the app. Make the Bible yours.

Editing a Verse

Simply click on the text of any verse. An editor window will open.

  • Text: Modify the scripture text itself.
  • Notes: Add personal study notes that appear alongside the verse.
  • Save: Click Save Changes to commit to your local file.

Names of God

In Settings, you can globally customize how Divine Names are rendered (e.g., Yahweh vs Jehovah, Yeshua vs Jesus). This is a display-only setting that applies instantly.

6. Data Safety (Backup)

Your data is precious. The app provides robust backup tools.

Manual Backup

Go to Settings > Backup & Restore.

  • Download JSON: Creates a full backup file.
  • Download CSV: Creates a spreadsheet-compatible file.

Automatic Cloud Backup

If configured, the app can automatically sync backups to a designated endpoint. Check the "Cloud Actions" section in settings for status.

8. Troubleshooting

"Replace All" button is missing?

The button only appears after you perform a text search. It is not available in pure reading mode.

Changes aren't saving?

Ensure the "My Bible" indicator is Green. If it is Red, your local file connection may have been lost. Go to Settings and Reconnect.

Search only finds results in Chapter 1?

This usually happens if you explicitly selected Chapter 1. Clear the Chapter selection (set to "Chapter...") or just re-select the Book to reset the Smart Scope.
