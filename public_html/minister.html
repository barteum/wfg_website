Be God's Messenger

"And the Good News about the Kingdom will be preached throughout the whole world so that all nations will hear it." Matthew 24:14

Walk For God (WFG) exists to make this ancient prophecy a modern reality. A Walk For God Messenger is a consecrated volunteer who executes the Great Commission of our Lord Jesus — one home at a time.

This is a personal contribution to the service of God, a ministry dedicated to ensuring that the Word reaches every single household within a defined route of approximately 1,000 homes every single week.

Messengers are motivated by a deep love for Scripture and empowered by the Holy Spirit to share the core message of the Gospel. This vital ministry requires an estimated time commitment of 3 to 6 hours per week depending on your area terrain.

The Benefits to You

  • Walk for Me-God Time daily and grow your faith
  • Walk for Health — best exercise on the planet
  • Walk for Purpose to minister the Good News
  • Walk for Souls and community in need
  • Walk for God and do His Will

Why the Letterbox?

The founders of WFG, leaders in the commercial logistics sector, understand the untapped power of Direct Mail for the Church.

Print and Letterbox Media consistently rank as the consumer's most preferred and most accepted advertising medium, outperforming TV, radio, newspapers, and even online marketing.

Commercial Dominance

Large corporations and SMEs utilize this consistent, aggressive medium to achieve saturation-level penetration into every home, every week, without fail.

The Church's Gap

Due to prohibitive costs, most Christian organizations lack the resources for a consistent, high-frequency Direct Mail campaign, preventing them from leveraging the medium's full potential.

WFG: Transforming the Landscape

We are fundamentally changing this dynamic. By eliminating the logistics administration and print subsidy barrier and streamlining operations for unprecedented growth, WFG empowers the Church Universal to penetrate every single home, every single week, with the Christian message.

Every home in your local area, city, and country possesses a letterbox — a neutral, physical gateway. Print media is retainable, powerful, and visible. It offers a tangible copy of God's Word that can be seen, read many times over, and easily shared within the family and community.

While person-to-person evangelism is vital, this method guarantees consistent penetration into every single home in your community with the retainable, printed Word of God.

Where Shall I Choose to Serve?

For logistical efficiency and personal stewardship, we encourage you to choose a postal pocket of 1,000 homes that is easily accessible to your residence. This proximity minimizes your travel costs, saves you time, and integrates your ministry seamlessly into your personal walking or exercise routine.

Serve Your Community: You can check the availability of specific zones by clicking on the Zones Tab in the top menu bar when Memberships start.
