Why Walk for God?

Walk for God is a powerful ministry dedicated to delivering God's word into homes around the world - at little or no cost to the Church. For a small donation of $25 for 1,000 Colour Pamphlets, you help reach the souls living in 1,000 homes every week with a message from Jesus. This strong touchpoint is connected to bigger digital messages that bring people into the light of our Saviour and advance God's Kingdom - a small contribution for so much good.

All spirit-anointed Christians are ministers of our Lord Jesus Christ. The followers of our Lord Jesus are spoken of as 'ministers of the good news,' as Paul was (Col 1:23); they are also "ministers of a new covenant," being in a covenant relationship with God; with Christ as our Mediator.

Becoming a Walk for God 'Local Area Messenger' (LAM) has the added benefit of giving you some healthy Me-and-God time, walking with God's Spirit, listening to the Bible or your favourite sermons. Volunteer some time to God and become a "Walk for God Messenger" to spread the message of the "Good News" to a pocket of 1,000 homes around your local area.

If you have a love for God's Word and would like to spread the good news greater and farther than ever before then join the walking revolution and consider becoming a Walk for God Messenger.

Our Commitment

Mission & Pledge

Our Mission

To take the Word of God to every home on Earth — 1,000 homes at a time. Whether reaching a local suburb or an entire nation, our spirit-filled Messengers volunteer to deliver the Good News weekly. Together, we offer this delivery service to the Body of Christ freely, ensuring that cost never hinders the spread of the Gospel.

Messenger's Pledge

Walk For God Messengers pledge to deliver all approved Christian material within their designated territory, on time and without fail. They further commit to supplying proof of delivery through the use of the MailPost GPS Tracking and Audit system, providing peace of mind and guaranteeing that all printed material has been successfully delivered.
